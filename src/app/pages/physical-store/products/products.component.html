Graphene OS

We use GrapheneOS that is further developed with our own security technologies that makes your phone even more secure. We´re working towards a new opensource OS - StellarOS which you can read more about here.

VPN BUILT ON OPENVPN

Our VPN-servers are owned by us. We do not store any data about which apps you use, which websites you use or how you use the internet.

BEFORE FIRST UNLOCK

If your phone is ever stolen, our unique software can activate a state known as 'BFU,' making it highly improbable for anyone to extract data from your device.

REMOTE WIPE

You have the option to perform a remote wipe of your phone at wipe.stellarsecurity.com. This action will erase all content on your device. If you require assistance with the wipe function, please don't hesitate to reach out to us, and we'll be glad to assist you.

STRONG PASSWORDS

Our software assists you in crafting exceptionally secure passwords. Employing a strong password stands as your foremost defense against hackers, significantly complicating data extraction. To enhance security even further, our phones are equipped with encrypted chips named M1 and M2, providing an additional layer of protection against data extraction.
